Executive SummaryThe Blank Registration Card is a purpose-built, static print document within the Kazi Resort HMS (Cubix HMS V-9.3.0). It is accessed by clicking the "Blank Registration Card" button — the sixth and final tab in the Room Registration workflow — which navigates to a standalone, print-optimised URL: /front_office/room-registration/blank-registration-card.The document is titled "Pre Registration Card" and functions as a standardised blank paper form that front-desk staff print and hand to arriving guests to complete by hand before or during check-in. It consolidates all essential guest identity, stay, payment, and contact information into a single A4-compatible page with 27 labeled data entry fields, 14 hotel policy clauses, a guest consent declaration, and two signature lines.Key business capabilities:PRINT BLANK FORM: Staff open the page and use the browser native Print function (Ctrl+P) to produce a blank, pre-formatted registration card for guest handwriting.HOTEL BRANDING: Every printed card carries the Kazi Resort logo, property name, address, hotline numbers, website, and email.ROOM IDENTIFICATION: A "Room No" field at the top-right links the completed card to a specific room assignment.GUEST IDENTITY CAPTURE: Four guest rows (Title / First name / Surname) allow up to four guests per registration.BOOKING REFERENCE: Reservation No. and Registration No. fields link the paper card to the digital HMS record.STAY AND ROOM DETAILS: Arrival/Departure dates, Room type, Pax count, Room tariff, Advance payment, and Payment mode.IDENTITY DOCUMENT CAPTURE: Country, Passport/NID No., Issue Date, Expiry Date, Date of Birth, Visa details for legal compliance.CONTACT AND CORPORATE INFO: Phone, Email, Home Address, Company name, Reference, and Remarks.POLICY ACKNOWLEDGEMENT: 14 hotel policy clauses printed on the card; guest signs to acknowledge them.DATA CONSENT: GDPR/privacy consent declaration above the signature lines.DUAL SIGNATURES: "Checked in By*" (staff) and "Guest Signature*" (guest) establish mutual accountability.CHECK-IN TIME CONFIRMATION: Physical check-in and check-out time fields at the bottom.The page is intentionally static — it contains no interactive form elements, no database queries, no user-specific data pre-fill, and no JavaScript. It produces an identical printed form for all users and all registration scenarios. Its sole actionable process from the system perspective is being navigated to and printed.2. Gaps and RecommendationsThe following gaps were identified through live audit of the Blank Registration Card page on April 16, 2026. Each gap includes a risk assessment and specific recommendation.Gap IDGap TitleDescriptionRisk LevelRecommendationGAP-001No In-App Print ButtonThe page has no dedicated print button. Staff must know to use browser Ctrl+P. This creates a training gap — new staff may not know how to print the card.LowAdd a prominent "Print Card" button (e.g., top-right, above the card) that calls window.print() via JavaScript. This makes the action explicit and user-friendly.GAP-002Hotel Contact Info is Hard-CodedHotel name, address, phone numbers, website, and email are hard-coded in the HTML template. Any change to hotel contact details (e.g. new phone number) requires a code change and deployment.MediumMove hotel contact information to the HMS property settings configuration table. The template should read these values dynamically from the database.GAP-003Policy Clauses and Penalty Amounts are Hard-CodedAll 14 policy clauses, including specific financial penalties (BDT 600 lost key, BDT 2,500/2,000 smoking), are hard-coded. Policy changes require code deployment.MediumCreate a "Registration Card Policy" settings module in HMS. Store policy clauses as configurable text records in the database, editable by hotel management from an admin panel.GAP-004No Auto-Print on Page LoadStaff must manually trigger print. If the sole purpose of this page is printing, an auto-print on page load (with a cancel option) would streamline the workflow.LowImplement optional auto-print: add window.print() on page load with a 500ms delay. Show a "Click to print" button as fallback. This reduces clicks from Ctrl+P to zero for staff.GAP-005Typo in Policy Clause 5Clause 5 reads: "All complains after check-out is not applicable or recivable." Contains two errors: "complains" should be "complaints" and "recivable" should be "receivable".LowCorrect the typo in the HTML template: change to "All complaints after check-out are not applicable or receivable."GAP-006Typo in Policy Clause 11Clause 11 reads: "Guests are not permited to carry weapons..." — "permited" should be "permitted".LowCorrect the typo: change to "permitted".GAP-007No Guest-Specific Pre-Fill OptionThe card is always completely blank. For guests with existing reservations, it would save time if the card could optionally pre-fill with known data (guest name, room, dates) from the reservation.MediumIntroduce a complementary "Filled Registration Card" view (or a "Print with Reservation Data" button on the Search tab) that pre-populates the card fields from a selected HMS reservation, printing a partially-completed card.GAP-008Manual Data Transcription RequiredThere is no mechanism to scan or auto-import data from the completed paper card into HMS. All guest-written data must be manually re-entered by staff, introducing transcription error risk.HighLong-term: integrate OCR (Optical Character Recognition) scanning or QR code capabilities. Short-term: implement a structured digital intake form (tablet/kiosk) guests can complete digitally at check-in.GAP-009No Check-In/Out Time Fields as Standard HoursThe standard check-in time (14:00:00 HRS) and check-out time (12:00:00 HRS) are stated in policy clause 12 but the physical "Check-In at...HRS" entry fields are separate and manual. Staff may write incorrect times.LowPre-print the standard times as default values (e.g., "Check-In at: 14:00 HRS" greyed) with space to override, reducing the chance of blank or incorrect time entries.GAP-0104-Guest Limit Hard-CodedThe card always shows exactly 4 guest rows. For a large family (5+ members) or a group registration, the card cannot accommodate more than 4 guests.LowMake the guest row count configurable via an HMS setting, or add a note "For additional guests, see attached supplementary card."GAP-011No Language/Localisation SupportThe card is English-only. For international guests unfamiliar with English, the policy clauses and field labels may be difficult to understand.MediumAdd a hotel setting to optionally print a bilingual card (English + one other language, e.g., Arabic, Chinese, or French) based on guest nationality or booking channel.GAP-012No Print Log or Audit TrailThere is no HMS record of when the Blank Registration Card was printed, by which staff member, or how many copies were produced.LowIf auto-print is implemented (GAP-004), log each print event to an audit table with timestamp, user ID, and session context. This supports security and accountability tracking.GAP-013Inconsistent "Hotline:" Label in HeaderThe address line reads "Hotline: Hotline: +8801894803511" — the word "Hotline" appears twice (once as a label, once repeated). This is a display bug.LowFix the template to remove the duplicate "Hotline:" prefix, rendering as: "Hotline: +8801894803511"9.
