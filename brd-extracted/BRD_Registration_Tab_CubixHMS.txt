1. Executive Summary
This Business Requirements Document (BRD) defines the detailed functional, data, UI, and business requirements for the Registration Tab — the first and primary tab within the Room Registration workflow of the Front Office module in Cubix HMS (Hotel Management System).
The Registration Tab is the entry point for all guest check-in activities in the hotel. It is the most data-intensive tab in the Room Registration wizard, responsible for capturing every critical piece of room assignment and billing information required to register a guest — whether the guest is a walk-in, linked to an existing reservation, or an in-house guest having their stay modified.
The tab is organized into three major functional sections: (1) the Room Registration Header section capturing booking-level metadata, (2) the Room Detailed Information section capturing per-room financial and stay details, and (3) the Additional Service section capturing supplementary services. Together, these sections drive the creation of a complete room registration record, enable room inventory management, and set up the financial parameters for the guest folio.
Scope of this BRD:
•  Module: Registration Tab (Tab 1 of Room Registration)
•  Parent System: Cubix HMS – Front Office
•  Primary Users: Front Desk Agents, Reservation Agents, Duty Managers, Night Auditors
•  Trigger: Walk-in guest check-in or reservation-linked room assignment
•  Output: Room registration record with room assignment, billing parameters, and supplementary service data saved to the HMS database
Business Objective:
To enable hotel front-desk staff to efficiently, accurately, and completely capture all room registration details — including room assignment, rate configuration, billing flags, occupancy, additional services, and stay metadata — in a single, guided interface that drives all downstream hotel operations including housekeeping, billing, night audit, and reporting.
2. Module Context & Positioning
The Registration Tab does not function as a standalone module. It is Tab 1 of 5 within the Room Registration wizard — the core operational workflow of the Front Office module in Cubix HMS. Data entered on this tab is the foundational dataset for all subsequent tabs and all downstream hotel operations.

4.6 Data Relationships
•  One Room Registration has zero or one reservation link (0 or 1 : 1 with Reservation)
•  One Room Registration has one or many Room Registration Detail records (1:N) — each room added is one row
•  One Room Registration has zero or many Additional Service records (0:N)
•  One Room Registration Detail links to one Room Type and one Room Number from inventory
•  One Room Registration links to one Currency, one Property, one Created-by User
•  One Room Registration may optionally link to one Listed Company
•  One Room Registration links to one Meal Plan (required) and one Reference (required)

8. Gaps & Recommendations
This section identifies functional gaps, usability shortcomings, and enhancement opportunities observed in the current implementation of the Registration Tab.
Gap ID
Area
Current Behavior
Gap / Issue
Recommendation
Priority
GAP-001
Draft / Auto-Save
No auto-save; all data is lost if the user closes the browser or the session times out before check-in.
Data loss risk during long registration sessions or connectivity issues.
Implement draft-save functionality: auto-save Registration Tab data to the server every 30 seconds or on navigation between tabs (as a draft/pending registration status).
High
GAP-002
Complimentary + House Use Mutual Exclusivity
Both "Complimentary" and "House Use" checkboxes can be checked simultaneously.
Logically conflicting stay types can be saved, causing downstream processing issues in billing and audit.
Enforce mutual exclusivity: checking one should automatically uncheck the other, with a clear tooltip or message.
High
GAP-003
Negotiated Rate Override Audit
A Negotiated Rate can be entered by any user without restriction.
Unauthorized rate changes bypass rate plan controls, risking revenue loss.
Require manager-level authentication (e.g., manager PIN or elevated session role) to enter or change the Negotiated Rate.
High
GAP-004
Discount Amount Cap Validation
No client-side validation prevents Discount Amount from exceeding Rack Rate when Discount Type is Fixed.
A discount greater than the rack rate would produce a negative Total Room Rent Amount.
Enforce client-side and server-side validation: if Discount Type = Fixed, Discount Amount must be ≤ Rack Rate. Display an inline error.
High
GAP-005
Room List Without Room Type
No prominent error is shown if the user tries to open the Room List modal before selecting a Room Type.
Poor UX; modal may show all rooms or an empty list, confusing the user.
Disable the Room List button until a Room Type is selected. Show a tooltip: "Please select a Room Type first."
Medium
GAP-006
Mandatory Room Enforcement
No UI feedback when Check-In is attempted with zero rooms in the summary table.
Users are blocked from checking in but receive no clear feedback about why.
Show a clear, field-level error near the summary table: "At least one room must be added before check-in."
High
GAP-007
Additional Service Date Constraints
No validation prevents Service From Date from being before the Check-In Date or Service To Date from being after the Departure Date.
Out-of-stay services can be added, causing billing inconsistencies.
Enforce: Service From Date ≥ Check-In Date AND Service To Date ≤ Departure Date. Display inline validation errors.
Medium
GAP-008
Pay For & Payment Mode Dependency on Company
Pay For and Payment Mode fields are present but may be accessible without a company being selected.
Staff can set Payment Mode without a company context, causing data integrity issues.
Strictly enforce that Payment Mode and Pay For fields are only enabled (and saved) when a Listed Company is selected.
Medium
GAP-009
Currency Impact Clarity
When currency is changed, it is unclear to the user which fields have been recalculated.
Staff may not notice rate changes after currency switch, especially in multi-room registrations.
Highlight recalculated fields (e.g., yellow flash/border animation) after currency change to draw attention to updated values.
Low
GAP-010
Room Type Typo in Service Master
The service "Additional Room Rrevenue" contains a typo (double 'r' in "Revenue").
Incorrect label visible on the registration form; may confuse staff and appears unprofessional.
Correct the label to "Additional Room Revenue" in the service master configuration.
Low
GAP-011
Total Nights Manual Override Logic
The "Checkout checkbox" next to Total Nights appears to allow manual override, but the behavior is undocumented.
If the override is not properly validated server-side, it could cause mismatched date/night records.
Document and test the Total Nights override checkbox behavior; add server-side validation to confirm that override Total Nights matches the date difference or triggers appropriate downstream adjustments.
Medium
GAP-012
No Summary of All Charges
There is no running total or billing summary panel showing: base rate + SC + VAT + City Charge + Additional Charges + all additional services.
Front-desk agents cannot see the total billing amount across all rooms and services without mentally summing values.
Add a "Billing Summary" panel at the bottom of the Registration Tab showing a clear breakdown and grand total of all charges across all added rooms and services.
Medium
GAP-013
Accessibility (A11Y)
No ARIA attributes observed on complex interactive elements (modals, checkboxes, disabled fields).
Screen reader users and visually impaired staff cannot effectively navigate the Registration Tab.
Add ARIA roles, aria-label, aria-required, aria-disabled, and aria-expanded attributes to all interactive form elements and modals. Validate against WCAG 2.1 AA.
Medium
GAP-014
Driver Accommodation Typo
Service master contains "Driver Accomodation" (missing one 'm' in 'Accommodation').
Typographical error in the Service Name dropdown, visible to front-desk staff.
Correct spelling to "Driver Accommodation" in the service master configuration.
Low
GAP-015
Mobile / Tablet Responsiveness
The multi-column layout of Room Detailed Information may not reflow properly on tablet screens.
Front-desk staff using tablets may experience overlapping fields or horizontal scrolling on the Registration Tab.
Test and validate responsive layout at 768px and 1024px breakpoints; use CSS media queries to ensure 2-column or 1-column reflow for tablet/mobile.
Medium
GAP-016
Confirmation Before Delete (Room Row)
No explicit confirmation dialog visible when the Delete button is clicked on a room row in the summary table.
Accidental room deletion can occur without user confirmation.
Implement a confirmation dialog: "Are you sure you want to remove Room [X]? This action cannot be undone without re-adding the room."
High
GAP-017
Meal Plan Required but Selectable Without Rate Context
Meal Plan is marked as required (*) but changing the Meal Plan does not dynamically update the room rate or charges.
If meal plan affects the room rate (e.g., BED & BREAKFAST includes breakfast charge), the static rate display does not reflect this.
Either (a) link Meal Plan selection to rate plan to update charges dynamically, or (b) clearly document that Meal Plan is for informational/operational routing only and does not affect the nightly rate shown on the form.
Medium
